What is Yin Yoga?

Yin Yoga is a meditative practice that targets deep connective tissues like fascia, ligaments, and joints. It involves holding poses for longer durations, typically 3-5 minutes, to enhance flexibility and promote mindfulness. Yin Yoga complements more active yoga styles and supports a balanced practice.

Key Yin Yoga Poses

1. Butterfly Pose

Sit with the soles of your feet together and let your knees fall outward. Gently lean forward to deepen the stretch.

2. Dragon Pose

Step one foot forward into a lunge position and rest your hands on the ground or a block. Hold to open the hips deeply.

3. Caterpillar Pose

Stretch your legs straight in front of you and fold forward over them, letting your head and neck relax.

4. Reclining Twist

Lie on your back, bring one knee across your body, and let it rest on the ground. Relax and breathe into the twist.
