Yoga Poses for Beginners

Start your yoga journey with simple, relaxing poses for better flexibility and mindfulness.

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

A foundational pose that improves posture and balance. Stand tall with feet together, shoulders relaxed, and arms by your side.



Benefits Of Tadasana:-

  • Improves posture

  • Improves circulation

  • Reduces stress and anxiety

Child's Pose (Balasana)

A resting pose that stretches the hips, thighs, and spine. Sit back on your heels and stretch your arms forward.



Benefits Of Balasana:-

  • Improved sleep

  • Back and spine health:

  • Menstrual discomfort

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Stretch your body by forming an inverted "V" shape. Keep your hands and feet grounded as you lift your hips.



Benefits Of Downward Dog:-

  • Improves flexibility

  • Relieves pain

  • Calms the mind

Cat-Cow Stretch

A gentle stretch for the spine. Alternate between arching your back (Cow) and rounding it (Cat) while on your hands and knees.



Benefits Of Cat-Cow Stretch:-

  • Better balance

  • Stimulated organs

  • Reduced belly fat
