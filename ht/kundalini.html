About Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini Yoga is a powerful blend of physical postures, breathing techniques, meditation, and chanting. It focuses on awakening the Kundalini energy located at the base of the spine and channeling it upward through the chakras to achieve self-awareness and enlightenment.

Key Kundalini Yoga Practices

1. Breath of Fire (Kapalabhati)

Quick, rhythmic breathing through the nose, energizing the body and mind while detoxifying the system.

2. Spinal Flex

Sit in an easy pose and gently flex your spine back and forth with synchronized breath, awakening the spine.

3. Ego Eradicator

Extend your arms upward at 60 degrees with Breath of Fire, clearing negative energy and promoting mental clarity.

4. Sat Kriya

Chant "Sat Nam" while interlocking your fingers and stretching your arms overhead, activating your inner energy.
