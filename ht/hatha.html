Hatha Yoga

Balance the body and mind through movement and breath.

About Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga is a traditional form of yoga focusing on physical postures (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama). Its goal is to create harmony between the body and mind, paving the way for a meditative state. Hatha Yoga is perfect for beginners and seasoned practitioners alike.

Key Hatha Yoga Poses

1. Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Stand tall with your feet together, arms at your sides, and focus on your breath to find balance.

2. Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Stand on one leg, place the opposite foot on your inner thigh, and bring your hands together in prayer pose.

3. Downward Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Form an inverted "V" shape with your body, stretching your arms and legs while engaging your core.

4. Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

Step one foot back, bend the front knee, and stretch your arms outward for strength and focus.
