About Power Yoga

Power Yoga is a vigorous, fitness-based approach to Vinyasa Yoga. It emphasizes strength, flexibility, and stamina, blending traditional yoga with modern exercise techniques. It's perfect for those seeking a full-body workout while maintaining a connection to the breath and mindfulness.

Key Power Yoga Poses

1. Plank Pose

Strengthen your core and build upper body endurance by holding a plank position with controlled breath.

2. Chair Pose

Engage your thighs and glutes as you hold a deep squat position, arms raised overhead.

3. Warrior III

Balance on one leg with your arms stretched forward, engaging your core for strength and stability.

4. Side Plank

Strengthen your obliques and improve balance by holding a side plank position on one arm.
